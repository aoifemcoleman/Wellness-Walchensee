Opened in 2022, we are Germany's first luxury mountain wellness lodge located in the heart of the Bavarian Alps.


Our mission is to provide a first-class respite, recuperation and rejuvination facility to all lovers of the outdoors, leaving you ready to take on your next adventure.

Why Stay With Us?

Guided Meditation

Our meditation classes are run by certified instructors, and are suitable for all those interested in strengthening their minds - both beginners and regular practitioners. The classes offer an opportunity to relax and learn techniques to ground ourselves amidst the pressures of daily life.

State-of-the-Art Sauna

Our Finnish-style sauna facility contains 2 sauna rooms - a smoke sauna and wood-burning sauna. The benefits of sauna-use are plentiful and include detoxification, muscle relaxation, improved immune function and stress reduction. After a session in the sauna, guests have the opportunity for a refreshing swim in the lake directly beside our facility.

Guided Hiking Tours

With the most beautiful mountain landscape and trails on our doorstep, we are delighted to offer a range of guided hikes with qualified mountain guides - suitable for beginners and seasoned hikers. A list of available tours can be found at reception during check-in, and can be signed-up for at an additional cost of €20 per participant.

Yoga Classes

We offer a variety of yoga classes which are run throughout each day by qualified instructors. The classes offered are Vinyasa, Restorative and Hatha yoga.

Spa

Our luxury spa facility contains 12 treatment rooms, with a team of massage therapists and aestheticians looking to help you feel your best self after your treatments of choice. A list of all available treatments can be found at reception upon check-in.
